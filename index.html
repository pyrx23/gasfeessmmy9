Treasury.

Having a healthy treasury creates many potential opportunities. The treasury will be seeded with 40 million FEE at launch and supplemented over time by a proportion of the internal FEE transfer fees incurred on token transfers. At launch, the treasury will be locked while a DAO is established to provide governance. Once unlocked and controlled by the DAO, there are multiple strategies and options the DAO can explore, including but not limited to using treasury funds for:

  • Token buybacks;
  • Token burns;
  • Increasing liquidity depth; and
  • Activities on other protocols on and/or chains (e.g. staking pools).
